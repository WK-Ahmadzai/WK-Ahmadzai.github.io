I completed an MSc in Agricultural Sciences (Agricultural Economics) at the University of Tsukuba. My research interests lie in agricultural economics, consumer behavior, food systems, and rural development, with particular emphasis on their social and ethical dimensions.

Research Interests

Consumer Preferences

I study how social, ethical, and product-related information shapes food choice, valuation, and decision-making using stated-preference and experimental approaches.

Food Systems and Development

I examine how agriculture and food systems influence livelihoods, food security, and rural development, particularly in developing-country contexts.

Social Impact and Agricultural Livelihoods

I investigate the social implications of agricultural systems, with emphasis on women’s empowerment, equity, and ethical dimensions of production and consumer behavior.
